Game Rules

How to Play:

  • Goal: Get as close to 21 as possible without going over (busting)
  • Card Values: 2-10 = face value, J/Q/K = 10, Ace = 1 or 11
  • Blackjack: An Ace and a 10-value card on the first two cards (pays 3:2)
  • Hit: Take another card
  • Stand: Keep your current hand
  • Double Down: Double your bet and receive exactly one more card
  • Split: If you have two cards of the same value, split them into two hands
  • Insurance: When dealer shows an Ace, you can bet half your original bet that dealer has blackjack
  • Dealer Rules: Dealer must hit on 16 and below, stand on 17 and above
